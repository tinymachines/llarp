rockchip

Rockchip target provides support for RK33xx and RK35xx SoCs. These are often used in set-top-boxes and small Raspberry-style single board computers. RK33xx SoCs offer DDR3/4 DRAM support and PCIe expansion and are useful as very powerful and low power routers. See also Rockchip
